# Launchframe — Launch Retro

## Pre-Launch Notes

The beta release process exposed one major template improvement: a dogfood example must validate as a real workspace, not only exist as loose launch notes.

## Lessons Captured

- Keep owner authorization and final approval gates explicit.
- Validate the dogfood workspace in CI.
- Treat newly-created HN accounts as a launch blocker for public posting.
- Keep Startup Pilot capture low-friction.
- Use signed browser automation for account consoles.
