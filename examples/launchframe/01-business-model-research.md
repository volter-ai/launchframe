# Launchframe — Comparable Business Model Research

## Current Posture

Launchframe is a free OSS CLI/template system under Apache-2.0. The public beta may collect Startup Pilot interest, but it does not collect payment or claim a packaged SaaS product.

## Comparable Pattern

Developer-tool OSS projects commonly keep the core tool free and monetize through support, hosted workflow, team features, enterprise onboarding, or services. Launchframe should not publish a concrete price until the owner approves an offer.

## Claims Allowed

| Claim | Evidence | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Free OSS CLI | Apache-2.0 repo and npm package | verified |
| Startup Pilot interest capture | live Tally form | verified |
| Enterprise/custom support may be discussed privately | owner-approved positioning | owner-approved |

## Claims To Avoid

| Claim | Why not |
|---|---|
| Published SaaS pricing | no public paid product exists |
| Enterprise-ready launch platform | unsupported by product evidence |
| Hosted workflow | current CLI is local scaffold/validation |

## Launch Implication

Use "free OSS CLI" publicly. Use Startup Pilot only as a low-friction interest capture, with no payment collected until fit is confirmed.
