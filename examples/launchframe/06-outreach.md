# Launchframe — Outreach

## Scope

No cold outreach is required for the beta release. HN/Reddit/social drafts are prepared, but direct outreach should wait until the public beta surfaces remain stable after launch.

## Short Description

Launchframe is an OSS CLI/template system for creating auditable launch workspaces for developer-tool launches.

## Avoid

- Vote solicitation.
- Comment coordination.
- Communities that prohibit promotional launch posts.
