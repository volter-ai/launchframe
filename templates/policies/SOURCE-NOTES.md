# Policy And Community Template Source Notes

These templates are structured from stable public documentation and open template sources. They are not legal advice.

## Sources To Check Before Use

- GitHub community health files and issue templates:
  - https://docs.github.com/communities/setting-up-your-project-for-healthy-contributions
  - https://docs.github.com/communities/setting-up-your-project-for-healthy-contributions/configuring-issue-templates-for-your-repository
  - https://docs.github.com/code-security/getting-started/adding-a-security-policy-to-your-repository
- Licenses:
  - https://choosealicense.com/
  - https://spdx.org/licenses/
- Code of conduct:
  - https://www.contributor-covenant.org/
- Policy examples to review for structure, not blind copy:
  - https://github.com/basecamp/policies
  - https://github.com/Automattic/legalmattic

## Operating Rules

- Copy exact license text only from authoritative license sources.
- Copy current Contributor Covenant text only from its official source.
- Fill privacy, terms, and telemetry templates from verified product behavior.
- Keep `TBD` in place rather than inventing a claim.
- Treat unresolved `TBD` policy items as launch blockers when public users rely on them.
- Get owner/legal review before publishing privacy or terms pages.
